Description of the Area

Downtown Lawrence comprises about twenty city blocks between South Park and the Kansas River. This area contains many shops, restaurants, and historic buildings, making it a central part of the cityâ€™s activity.

East of downtown is an area known as East Lawrence. It includes a mix of residential neighborhoods and small businesses, with a strong sense of local community.

West of downtown is Old West Lawrence, which is primarily residential and known for its older homes and tree-lined streets.

Southwest of Old West Lawrence is the University of Kansas. The campus occupies a large area and plays a major role in the culture, economy, and daily life of the city.
